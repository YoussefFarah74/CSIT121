BBQ chicken platter

By; Tom Kerrige

Enjoy the Taste

Prep Time: 40mins

CooK Time 1hr 20mins

Best servered @bbcgoodfood


Ingridients
  • 1 whole chicken , jointed, or 8 bone-in chicken pieces (we used legs, breasts, thighs and wings)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic granules
  • 2 tbsp runny honey
  • 2 tbsp cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp light brown soft sugar
  • 200g American mustard

Recipe
  1. To make the spice rub, mix the salt, smoked paprika and garlic granules together in a large bowl. Add the chicken pieces and toss well to coat. Transfer to the fridge to chill for at least 2 hrs.

  2. To make the BBQ sauce, tip the honey, vinegar, sugar and mustard into a pan and bring to the boil, stirring to dissolve the sugar. Remove from the heat and leave to cool.

  3. Heat the oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4. Pour half of the the BBQ sauce into a large bowl, add the seasoned chicken pieces and toss to coat. Spread the chicken out on a baking tray and roast for 1 hr, occasionally basting with more BBQ sauce, until the chicken is cooked through and sticky. Serve with truffled mac 'n' cheese, onion rings and coleslaw if you like.
