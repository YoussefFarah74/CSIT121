Delicious Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Beat egg and vanilla in a small bowl, and stir into flour mixture until well blended.
  5. Roll dough out on a floured surface and cut into desired shapes.
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until edges are lightly browned.
